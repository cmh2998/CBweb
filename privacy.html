Who we are

Cadence Black is the data controller for this website. For anything relating to your personal data, contact us at info@cadenceblack.co.uk.

What we collect and why

Newsletter: if you sign up, we collect your email address, used solely to send you the newsletter. The lawful basis is your consent, given when you subscribe. We never sell or share your email with anyone else, and every email includes an unsubscribe link. Withdrawing consent is as easy as giving it.

Event registrations and enquiries: if you register interest in an event or contact us, we use the details you provide only to respond to you and administer that event.

Shop orders: when the shops go live, order details (name, delivery address, contact email) will be used only to fulfil your order. Payment details are handled by the payment provider and never stored by us.

Sensitivity, discretion, and this community

We understand that an association with this site can itself be sensitive personal information. We treat every piece of data with that in mind: mailing lists are kept private, sender names and packaging for any orders are discreet, and we will never disclose that someone is a subscriber, customer, or community member.

How long we keep your data

Newsletter emails are kept until you unsubscribe, at which point they are deleted from the mailing list. Enquiry and event data is kept only as long as needed to administer the event or conversation. Order records are kept as required for tax and accounting law.

Where your data lives

The newsletter is managed through [email provider, e.g. Mailchimp or Buttondown], who process your email address on our behalf under their own GDPR-compliant terms.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access the data we hold about you, correct it, have it deleted, restrict or object to how it's used, and take it elsewhere. To exercise any of these, email info@cadenceblack.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not use advertising or tracking cookies. Your accessibility preferences and shopping bag are stored only in your own browser and never leave your device.
