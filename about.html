About

Cadence Black

Latex maker, educator, and unapologetic advocate for a kink-positive world. This is the story so far.

The short version

Craft first, judgment never

Cadence Black started as a love of latex and grew into a small world: a bespoke atelier in DeCadence, a charity shop in ReCadence, a podcast with her partner KG, and a growing library of education for anyone curious enough to ask.

Everything here is built on the same three ideas — consent, craftsmanship, and the belief that kink deserves to be talked about with the same care as anything else people love.

Values

What this is built on

Consent, always

Craftsmanship

Whether you're brand new or been in the scene for decades, you're welcome here exactly as you are.
